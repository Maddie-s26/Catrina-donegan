Catrina Donegan

  1. Do you consent to giving your input on this survey?

    2. Yes

  2. Do you have an experienced issue with the the NHS? If so, what was it?

    • General practice
    • So busy, not enough time and not enough nurses so patient care is deteriorating - it also means that patients often abuse the nurses because waiting times are so long
    • Sometimes you don't get appreciated as a nurse by comparison with other nurses, disheartening
    • Overworked which makes you anxious which means you can't give your all for the patient

  3. How do you feel about waiting times at hospitals?

    • They're disastrous - far too long
    • Pandemic has made it so much worse because there is a huge backlog
    • Makes it more stressful for people working and anxiety levels rise between staff members and patients and it sort of spirals downward

  4. Are hospital staff overworked? Why/why not?

    • Yes definitely, 100%
    • Understaffing
    • Not enough staff in all departments
    • Lack of appreciation, especially for nurses, means that people feel less inclined to take up nursing - as well with a complete underpayment
    • Rising costs of living means that becoming a nurse is quite undesirable - has a massive impact on their family life and mental health

  5. Would a wearable device that monitors health be invasive? Why/ why not

    • No, it is a very good idea
    • It's done on an individual basis - catering to individual needs
    • Private and confidential - know when to go and when not to go and encourage people who wouldn't usually seek medical device to go and seek it if they need it. There are 2 sides to the coin

  6. What's your opinion on your health data being sent to the NHS?

    • If its done securely, it's okay
    • The system has to be really rigid because there are always blips
    • People dealing with the data need to be trained to a high standard to avoid mistakes and what not

  7. What is your overall opinion on the NHS? Why is this?

It's by no means perfect, but it is a very good free service for all the patients in this country. There is a lot of room for improvement with it.
